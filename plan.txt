TEKUNIK SUBSCRIPTION PLANS
==========================

1. BRONZE PLAN ($9/Month)
-------------------------
The Bronze plan is designed for individuals and personal projects looking for a quick and professional online presence.

Key Features:
- 1 Personal Subdomain (e.g., yourname.tekunik.com)
- Standard Templates Access
- "Powered by Tekunik" Branding
- Community Support
- Fixed Layout Options

Note: This plan does NOT support Custom Domains.


2. SILVER PLAN ($19/Month) - MOST POPULAR
-----------------------------------------
The Silver plan is ideal for growing businesses and professionals who want a more customized and branded experience.

Key Features:
- Custom Domain Support (e.g., www.yourbusiness.com)
- Premium Templates Access
- No Platform Branding (Remove "Powered by Tekunik")
- Advanced Analytics Dashboard
- Priority Email Support
- Enhanced Customization Controls


3. GOLD PLAN ($49/Month)
------------------------
The Gold plan is our top-tier solution for enterprises and high-traffic sites requiring maximum power and flexibility.

Key Features:
- Unlimited Custom Domains
- Exclusive "Gold-Only" Templates
- Full White-label Solution (See definition below)
- API Access for Integrations
- 24/7 VIP Support
- Advanced SEO Tools
- Priority Server Resources

WHAT IS A "FULL WHITE-LABEL SOLUTION"?
--------------------------------------
A White-label solution allows you to use the Tekunik platform to build sites but present them to the world as completely your own. 

- Total Branding Removal: All references to "Tekunik" are removed from the source code, footers, and meta tags.
- Custom Client Experience: If you are an agency building for clients, they will see your logo and branding in their management portal, not ours.
- Custom Favicons & Assets: Complete control over all platform assets to ensure a 100% consistent brand identity.
- Professional Edge: Your clients and visitors will never know you used a platform to build the site, making it appear as a high-end, custom-coded project.
